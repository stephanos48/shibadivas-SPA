Wait List


If you found us at a time when we do not have any puppies available, then please feel free to join our wait list. We provide this service on a first come, first serve basis.


How does it work?


It works as follows:

1. Send an email to shibadivaspuppies@gmail.com requesting a spot on the waiting list.
- Please include all relevant contact info in your email.
- If you have any preferences (boy or girl, sesame or red, etc), please specify in the email

2. I will then review your email and respond with your wait list number and estimated litter date.

3. I will periodically email the updated wait list out to waiting fur-parents (i.e. at a later date, I am also working on a way to add it to the website)

4. Once your number has come up, we will contact you with the good news and at that time you will be able to place a deposit down on your fur-baby.
- Once the deposit is made, the puppy is held just for you until he/she is ready to make the journey to your location.
- Per our terms, deposits are non-refundable.
